In the past year, we have done a research paper about the future of the printed world.

The research question was:
Will the two medias continue to live on or will one dominate the other? What will the implications be? Which factors/ ethnic differences could make people more likely to choose one over the other?

A Graph of total circulation of U.S. daily newspapers in the years 1940 - 2020

Considering this information, it seems that in the next 35 years newspapers estimated circulation will go down by 90% !!

We conducted a survey where we asked questions such as:

  • Do the participants read printed newspapers?
  • Did they visit a news site recently?
  • Which news format they prefer?

The survey answers:

  • Only 10% of teenagers under the age of 18 said that they have read a newspaper recently
  • A crushing majority of people visited a news site in the last 2 days
  • More than 65% explicitly said that they prefer electronic news over printed newspapers
